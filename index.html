Everything you need to know about the Coding Club and how to get involved

Joining is easy! Fill out our application form online. We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Once you submit the form, we'll get in touch with you about upcoming meetings and events.

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced programmers. Our community is built on learning together and supporting each other's growth. We offer workshops and mentoring to help you get started.

We work with a variety of languages and technologies depending on project needs.
